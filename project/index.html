About Kya Kya Culinary Street

Kya Kya Culinary Street is a vibrant night market in the heart of Surabaya, offering a wide array of traditional Chinese-Indonesian dishes and local delicacies. It's a feast for the senses, with aromatic spices, sizzling woks, and the bustling energy of the city at night.
